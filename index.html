Fun Facts About Crossaints


  • Croissants originated in Austria
  • Marie Antoinette adored them
  • The kipferl came first, the crossaint's ancestor. Kipferl can be dated back to at least the 13th century
  • They are sweet treats. Traditionally was served at breakfast with frangipane or chocolate fillings or jam and butter. They became a staple in French bakeries in 1920s.
  • They got savory in the 1970s. Crossaints were adapted into sandwiches and evolved from traditional sweet fillings into savory versions mostly filled with cheese, ham, and tomatoes.
  • Crossaints became fast food in the late 20th century. Pre-made frozen dough was introduced to the supermarkets. Sara Lee introduced a frozen croissant to America in 1981, Burger King followed with crossant breakfast sandwiches and the Mac Crossaint wasn't far behind.
